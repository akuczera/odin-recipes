Vegan Pasta Al Limone

Description

Say hello to the spring pasta of your dreams: Pasta Al Limone!

The sauce is incredibly velvety and creamy and perfectly coats al dente pasta. This dish is so delicious you'll be going back for seconds (or thirds), trust us.

It's the perfect weeknight meal for spring and beyond! Enjoy as an entree or side with a salad, roasted vegetables, or other Italian-inspired dishes.
