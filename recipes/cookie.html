1-Bowl Chocolate Peppermint Cookies

Description

These chocolate peppermint cookies are crispy on the edges, fudgy in the middle, plus super chocolaty and perfectly sweet and minty!

They're the perfect healthier dessert to celebrate the holiday season, bring to cookie parties, or gift to family and friends. Dip them in hot chocolate or another cozxy drink and wrap yourself in a warm blanket for al the holiday feels!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp flaxseed meal (ground flax seeds)
  • 5 Tbsp water
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil (use refined for less coconut flavor)
  • 1/3 cup coconut sugar (or organic cane sugar)
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp peppermint extract
  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/3 cup tapioca flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan semisweet or dark chocolate chips
  • 2-4 Tbsp crushed candy canes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (176 C) and line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
  2. To a large mixing bowl, add flax meal and water to create flax eggs. Let stand for 5 minutes to thicken.
  3. To the flax eggs, add melted coconut oil, coconut (or cane) sugar, vanilla extract, and peppermint extract. Whisk well to combine. The mixture should have the texture of caramel. To the wet ingredients, add almond flour, tapioca flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Mix the wet and dry ingredients together with a spatula until thoroughly combined and there are no streaks of flour left remaining. Add in the chocolate chips and stir again. It should be a thick, scoopable batter.
  4. Using a small (~1 Â½ Tbsp) cookie scoop (we like this one) or spoon, scoop out 12 equally sized cookies and place them onto the prepared baking sheet. Flatten the cookies slightly so there is a place for the crushed candy canes to go. Onto each cookie, sprinkle 1/2 -1 teaspoon of crushed candy cane. Press down gently to help the candy canes stick to the cookies.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes. The candy cane will be melted and the cookies slightly soft. Let cool at least 10 minutes before enjoying!
  6. Best when fresh. Store leftovers covered at room temperature for 1-2 days, in the refrigerator 4-5 days, or in the freezer for up to 1 month.
