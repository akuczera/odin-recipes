1-Bowl Chocolate Peppermint Cookies

Description

These chocolate peppermint cookies are crispy on the edges, fudgy in the middle, plus super chocolaty and perfectly sweet and minty!

They're the perfect healthier dessert to celebrate the holiday season, bring to cookie parties, or gift to family and friends. Dip them in hot chocolate or another cozxy drink and wrap yourself in a warm blanket for al the holiday feels!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp flaxseed meal (ground flax seeds)
  • 5 Tbsp water
  • 1/4 cup melted coconut oil (use refined for less coconut flavor)
  • 1/3 cup coconut sugar (or organic cane sugar)
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp peppermint extract
  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/3 cup tapioca flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 cup vegan semisweet or dark chocolate chips
  • 2-4 Tbsp crushed candy canes
